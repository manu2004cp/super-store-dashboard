Features Business Problem: Retailers often struggle to track and forecast sales performance by customer segment, region, or product‚Äîmaking it difficult to optimize campaigns and inventory.

Goal of the Dashboard: To provide interactive, actionable analytics and reliable sales forecasts for improved planning, marketing, and profitability.

Walkthrough of Key Visuals:

Top KPIs: Total Sales, Total Quantity, Total Profit, Avg. Delivery Time, Sales/Profit by State, City, Segment, Category

Sales & Profit by Month: Dual-year comparison for trend analysis.

Sales by Payment & Ship Mode: Breakdown by COD, Online, Card, and by delivery options.

Sales by Segment & Category: Segment filter and bar charts for product/business insight.

Sales Forecasting: Future trend projection using historical sales data (with confidence bands). Helps set revenue targets and anticipate demand fluctuations.

Interactive Filters: Drilldown by region, state, city, segment or time for in-depth exploration.

Business Impact & Insights: Improved Decision-Making: Identify high-performing products, profitable regions and peak months.

Strategic Planning: Plan promotions for festivals and holidays based on occasion-wise spikes.

Targeted Marketing: Insights to launch targeted campaigns in top-performing and emerging markets.

Operational Efficiency: Optimize shipping and payment modes based on customer preferences.

Forecasting: Provides actionable forward-looking metrics for inventory and sales planning.
